Welcome to Root Tronics Technologies

Root Tronics Technologies is a leading IT Software product Engineering services and consulting company. We are capable of implementing complex business with secure, durable and scalable service. We expertise in helping in order to make highly valuable business to our clients.

Established in 2018, Root Tronics Technologies specializes in providing Software product Engineering Services, & Solutions and technology modernization, cloud enablement, data management, automation and assurance services that help customers get competitive edge. Root Tronics Technologies Consulting is India's leading Search Selection & Software Engineering Product firm. Our consulting team is the most experienced, and amongst the largest, in the ASEAN region. We provide services to many of the world’s leading companies and financial institutions. We are committed to creating positive long term outcomes for both our clients and our candidates.

We practice ethical business and pride ourselves in our Partnership Engagement Model which is also an industry differentiator for us. Our clients vouch for us and our teams’ dedication towards making each engagement successful and profitable. We pride ourselves over the absolute commitment and passion of our team to ensure customer satisfaction.

Root Tronics Technologies can help you navigate today’s world of fast-growing technological complexity, ever-rising customer expectations and rapidly changing business environments. Through innovation, in-depth industry expertise and dedicated onshore and offshore teams of experts, we provide the capabilities, resources and experience to guide your digital development. We offer strategic yet practical advice, from blueprint to delivery and beyond.

We are a global IT company with excellence in consulting,product Engineering, outsourcing, infrastructure and digital solutions and services. We collaborate with companies to fulfill their ever-evolving IT needs and support their business with our enabling solutions to strengthen their current capabilities and build pathways for sustainable growth.
